Dinosaur game

This is my second game using JavaScript. But this time I have implemented HTML and CSS. There are two moving characters inte the game. A rich that moves across the screen from right to left. And a dinosaur that jumps over the rock. The game is all about surviving and jump over the rich without touch the rock. You get points throughout the game. The rock will accelerate at 90p and 200p. You win the game at 300p. It was a bit tricky in the beginning to connect CSS and HTML to JavaScript. But it got easier throughout the project.
